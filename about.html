About
Store

Bays Liquors is a liquor store located in Hampton Bays, NY. It has been family-owned since 2010, and is located in the movie theater shopping center. We are commited to keeping a clean, friendly store and making sure that our customers enjoy their visit.

Wine

We carry a large variety of wine. This includes national, well-known brands as well as local wines and hidden gems. Whether you are looking for higher-end wines or value wines in jug size, we have you covered.

Liquor

You can get all of your favorite liquor brands at Bays Liquors, and at competitive prices. If we don't have it, just ask us and we will try to get it for you.
